Space Invaders

The goal of our game is to shoot down the aliens that are trying to invade our world. You can move the character using the left and right keys and then using your mouse to aim, you can press the top arrow key to shoot lasers at the aliens. You have three lives in case an alien hits your character and at the end, depending on your score, you will be awarded a medal.

4.1.4 Rabbits, Grasses, Weed

In this project we looked into a model that simulated an ecosystem which consisted of rabbits, grasses, and weeds. We were able to observe how a population of rabbits was affected through the amount of vegetation that was available. If there was too much grass and weeds available, the bunnies would overpopulate, however, the bunny population would go extinct if there was too little for them to eat.
